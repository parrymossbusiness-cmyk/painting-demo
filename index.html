Ask us directly
What services do you provide?+

Allstar Painting provides interior painting, exterior painting, cabinet & trim painting, and drywall repair & surface prep for homes in Little Rock, Arkansas.

How do I request a quote?+

Call or text (501) 555-0142. Describe your rooms or exterior area, the color or finish you're going for, and any repairs needed. Photos can help start the conversation.

Can you color-match my existing walls or cabinets?+

In many cases, yes. Bring a paint chip, can label, or photo of your current color so we can match or recommend the closest option before your quote.

Do you clean up after the work?+

Cleanup is one of the most common compliments in our customer reviews. We mask thoroughly, use drop cloths throughout, and expect the job site to look better than we found it.

How does weather affect exterior painting scheduling?+

Exterior paint needs dry surfaces and moderate temperatures to cure properly, so Little Rock's rain and humidity can shift a start date or add a day. We build weather buffers into every exterior estimate and keep you posted if a schedule needs to move.
